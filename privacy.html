Introduction

CognifAI is an educational application that generates flashcards from user-provided topics using artificial intelligence.

Information We Process

When using CognifAI, you may enter topics, questions, or concepts that are submitted to an AI provider for flashcard generation.

How Information Is Used

  • Generate educational flashcards
  • Provide core application functionality
  • Return AI-generated learning content

Data Storage

Generated flashcards are stored locally on your device. CognifAI does not require account creation.

Third-Party Services

CognifAI uses third-party AI services to generate flashcards. User prompts may be transmitted to these providers for processing.

Advertising & Tracking

CognifAI does not use advertising networks and does not track users across apps or websites.
